## Description
Flowboard transforms how you manage projects and daily tasks with beautiful, intuitive kanban boards designed for iPhone and iPad.

Whether you are organizing work projects, tracking personal goals, or planning your week, Flowboard gives you a clear visual overview of everything on your plate. Move cards between columns with a simple drag, set due dates and priorities, and never miss a deadline again.

KEY FEATURES

Kanban Boards
Create unlimited boards for work, home, or any project. Customize columns to match your workflow and move cards with smooth drag-and-drop gestures.

Natural Language Input
Type "Finish report by Friday" and Flowboard automatically sets the title, due date, and priority. Adding tasks has never been faster.

Calendar View
See all your deadlines in a clean monthly calendar. Quickly spot busy weeks and reschedule tasks with a tap.

Today View
Focus on what matters right now. Today View shows tasks due today, overdue items, and your daily progress at a glance.

Smart Priorities
Assign four priority levels to any card. Color-coded badges make it easy to spot urgent items across your boards.

Custom Tags
Create tags with custom names and colors to organize cards your way. Filter by tag to find related tasks instantly.

iCloud Sync
Your boards stay up to date across all your devices with seamless iCloud synchronization. Start on your iPhone, continue on your iPad.

Dark Mode
Flowboard looks great in both light and dark modes, adapting to your system preferences automatically.

Widgets
Add a Today's Tasks widget to your Home Screen for quick access to your most important items without opening the app.

Onboarding
Get started in seconds with a guided walkthrough that shows you everything Flowboard can do.
